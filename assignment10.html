Save Animals & Birds

It's important to protect our wildlife. Every year, thousands of species are endangered due to habitat loss, pollution, and climate change.

Endangered Species

Currently, there are > 16,000 species worldwide that are endangered, with the number increasing every year.

Every day, more than 100 species become extinct, and it's estimated that by 2050, this number could be > 10,000 per year.

Conservation Efforts

Many organizations are working tirelessly to protect endangered species. By supporting these organizations, we can make a difference.

It's important to raise awareness about the importance of wildlife conservation and to take action to protect our planet's biodiversity.

Take Action

There are many ways you can help save animals and birds:

  • Support conservation organizations
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste and pollution
  • Volunteer for wildlife conservation projects
  • Spread awareness about endangered species

Number of Species at Risk

Number of species at risk: < 20,000.

Conclusion

It's up to all of us to protect our planet's wildlife. By taking action today, we can ensure a better future for all living beings.

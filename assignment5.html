My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Baahubali
    • Year: 2015
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati
    • Description: An epic tale of a prince's quest to reclaim his throne from an evil usurper.
  2. Avatar
    • Year: 2009
    • Main Actors: Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana
    • Description: A paraplegic Marine is sent to the moon Pandora on a unique mission but becomes torn between following orders and protecting the world he feels is his home.
  3. RRR
    • Year: 2022
    • Main Actors: N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan
    • Description: A fictional story about two legendary revolutionaries and their journey away from home before they started fighting for their country in the 1920s.
  4. Titanic
    • Year: 1997
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet
    • Description: A seventeen-year-old aristocrat falls in love with a kind but poor artist aboard the luxurious, ill-fated R.M.S. Titanic.
  5. Interceptor
    • Year: 2022
    • Main Actors: Elsa Pataky, Luke Bracey
    • Description: An Army captain must use her years of tactical training and military expertise to save humanity from sixteen nuclear missiles launched at the U.S. as a violent coordinated attack simultaneously threatens her remote missile interceptor station.
